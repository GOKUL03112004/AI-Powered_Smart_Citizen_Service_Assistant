TRADE LICENSE APPLICATION GUIDE

Overview:
A Trade License is a certificate issued by the local municipal authority that permits an individual or business entity to carry on a specific trade or business within a particular area. It ensures that the business activities comply with safety norms and municipal regulations.

Who Needs a Trade License:
- Shopkeepers and retail business owners
- Restaurant and food business operators
- Manufacturing units
- Service providers (salons, repair shops, etc.)
- Wholesale traders
- Any commercial establishment operating within municipal limits

Types of Trade Licenses:
1. General Trade License - For standard commercial establishments
2. Food Trade License - For food-related businesses
3. Industrial License - For small-scale manufacturing units
4. Hawker License - For mobile street vendors

Documents Required:
1. Filled application form
2. Identity proof of the applicant (Aadhaar, PAN Card, Passport)
3. Address proof of the business premises
4. Proof of ownership or rental agreement of the premises
5. Recent passport-size photographs (2 copies)
6. NOC from property owner (if rented)
7. Site plan/layout of the business premises
8. GST Registration Certificate (if applicable)
9. Partnership deed or incorporation certificate (for companies)
10. Food Safety License (for food businesses - FSSAI)

Application Process:
Step 1: Visit the local Municipal Corporation or Urban Local Body (ULB) office
Step 2: Obtain the trade license application form
Step 3: Fill the form with accurate business details
Step 4: Attach all required documents
Step 5: Pay the prescribed license fee
Step 6: Inspection by municipal authorities (may be required)
Step 7: Receive trade license upon approval

Online Application:
- Access the State Government's business portal or municipal website
- Create a business account
- Fill the digital application form
- Upload all documents
- Pay fees through net banking/UPI/credit card
- Track application status online
- Download e-license upon approval

License Fees (varies by state and business type):
- Small shops (up to 50 sq ft): Rs. 500-1000/year
- Medium establishments (50-200 sq ft): Rs. 1000-3000/year
- Large establishments (above 200 sq ft): Rs. 3000-10000/year
- Food businesses: Additional Rs. 500-2000/year

Validity and Renewal:
- Trade License is valid for 1 year (April 1 to March 31)
- Must be renewed annually before expiry
- Late renewal attracts penalty of 25% additional fee
- Non-renewal may result in business closure notices

Penalties for Operating Without License:
- Fine of Rs. 5,000 to Rs. 50,000
- Sealing of business premises
- Criminal prosecution in repeated violations

Important Notes:
- Different trades require different licenses (e.g., restaurants need FSSAI license additionally)
- The license must be prominently displayed at the business premises
- Any change in business activity requires a fresh application
- Transfer of license requires prior approval from the municipal authority

Contact: Local Municipal Corporation Office or State Business Portal
