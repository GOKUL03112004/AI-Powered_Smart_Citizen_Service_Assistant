FARMER WELFARE SCHEME (PM-KISAN SAMMAN NIDHI & RELATED SCHEMES)

Overview:
The Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) is a central government scheme that provides direct income support to all landholding farmer families in India. Additionally, several complementary farmer welfare schemes exist to provide comprehensive support to the agricultural community.

PM-KISAN SCHEME:

Eligibility:
- All landholding farmer families (having cultivable land)
- Small and marginal farmers (primary beneficiaries)
- Annual income below Rs. 2 lakh from non-agricultural sources
- The farming family should have the land in their name
- Age: 18 years and above

Exclusion Criteria:
- Income taxpayers
- Retired/Former government employees drawing pension above Rs. 10,000/month
- Former or sitting Members of Parliament/State Legislature/Ministers
- Retired employees of Central/State PSUs and Government autonomous bodies
- Professionals like doctors, engineers, lawyers, chartered accountants

Financial Benefit:
- Rs. 6,000 per year (Rs. 2,000 every four months)
- Amount directly credited to bank account via DBT

Documents Required:
1. Aadhaar Card (mandatory)
2. Land ownership documents (Khasra/Khatauni/Land Patta)
3. Bank account details linked to Aadhaar
4. Mobile number for OTP verification
5. Passport-size photograph

Application Process:
Step 1: Visit nearest Common Service Centre (CSC) or Gram Panchayat
Step 2: Provide Aadhaar and land records
Step 3: Fill PM-KISAN registration form
Step 4: Verify mobile number with OTP
Step 5: Application forwarded to state for verification
Step 6: Benefits start after state-level approval

Online Self-Registration:
- Visit pmkisan.gov.in
- Click "New Farmer Registration"
- Enter Aadhaar number and captcha
- Fill personal and land details
- Submit and receive registration number

PRADHAN MANTRI FASAL BIMA YOJANA (PMFBY - CROP INSURANCE):

Eligibility:
- All farmers growing notified crops
- Both loanee and non-loanee farmers
- Sharecroppers and tenant farmers also eligible

Premium Rates:
- Kharif crops: 2% of sum insured
- Rabi crops: 1.5% of sum insured
- Horticulture/Commercial crops: 5% of sum insured

KISAN CREDIT CARD (KCC):

Eligibility:
- Farmers with land (own or leased)
- Age: 18-75 years
- Share croppers, oral lessees, and tenant farmers

Benefits:
- Revolving credit facility
- Interest rate: 7% per annum (2% subvention + 3% prompt repayment incentive)
- Credit limit based on land holding and crops
- Also covers post-harvest expenses and allied activities

SOIL HEALTH CARD SCHEME:

Purpose: Provide soil health information to farmers to help improve crop yield

Benefits:
- Free soil testing
- Crop-specific fertilizer recommendations
- SMS alerts for soil health status
- Helps reduce unnecessary fertilizer use

PM KISAN MAAN DHAN YOJANA (FARMER PENSION):

Eligibility:
- Small and marginal farmers aged 18-40 years
- Agricultural land below 2 hectares
- Not covered under NPS, ESIC, EPFO, etc.

Benefit:
- Monthly pension of Rs. 3,000 after age 60

Contribution:
- Age 18: Rs. 55/month
- Age 29: Rs. 100/month
- Age 40: Rs. 200/month
- Equal contribution by Central Government

RASHTRIYA KRISHI VIKAS YOJANA (RKVY):

Purpose: Encourage states to increase public investment in agriculture

Benefits:
- Infrastructure development in agriculture
- Technology adoption support
- Market linkage support
- Value chain development

Helpline: Kisan Call Center - 1800-180-1551 (Toll Free, 24x7)
